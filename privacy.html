Privacy Protocol

Operational guidelines for data integrity and client confidentiality.

Data Encryption & Security

GASA TECH (PTY) LTD utilizes advanced encryption standards for all client sensitive data. Our internal systems are isolated from public nodes to prevent unauthorized access during the development lifecycle.

Information Collection

We collect specific data points necessary for digital infrastructure deployment, including but not limited to:

  • Corporate identity and branding assets
  • System architecture requirements
  • Strategic contact nodes (Email, Phone)
  • Deployment analytics and traffic logs

POPIA Compliance

In accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa, we ensure that all personal information is processed lawfully, transparently, and only for the purposes agreed upon during the project initialization phase.

Third-Party Policy

GASA TECH strictly prohibits the sale or unauthorized sharing of client data. Information is only shared with verified infrastructure partners (e.g., hosting providers) required to keep your system online.
