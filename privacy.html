Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 16, 2026

GASA TECH (PTY) LTD is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle personal information in strict accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary for us to provide our digital engineering services. This includes details provided via our contact forms such as your name, business name, email address, and phone number.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

Your data is processed solely for the purposes of:

  • Responding to project enquiries and consultation requests.
  • Delivering technical services and maintenance.
  • Invoicing and administrative requirements.

3. Data Security & Storage

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, or destruction of your personal information. Your data is stored on secure servers and is never sold to third parties.

4. Your Rights

In terms of POPIA, you have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your personal information held by GASA TECH at any time.
