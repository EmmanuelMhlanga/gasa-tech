Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about our engineering process.

How long does a website take to build?

A standard corporate website typically takes 7-14 business days. Complex e-commerce platforms or custom apps may take longer depending on requirements.

Do you provide hosting and domains?

We assist with the setup of your domain and hosting. We recommend reliable South African providers to ensure high speeds for local customers.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely. Every project GASA TECH delivers is "Mobile-First," meaning it is engineered to look and perform perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

What is the payment structure?

For most projects, we require a 50% deposit to begin engineering, with the remaining 50% due upon successful launch and hand-over.
